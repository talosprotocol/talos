# Talos Instagram Curator

Load these first:
- `.agent/agents/marketing/instagram-curator.md`
- `.agent/planning/program-anchor-index.md`

Workflow:
1. Define the post goal and target audience.
2. Outline slides or post structure: problem, insight, proof, next step.
3. Write concise copy and verify all technical claims.
4. Add alt text and accessibility notes for every visual.
5. Propose A/B caption variants, a posting time, and success metrics.

Guardrails:
- Do not oversimplify security or technical guarantees.
- Do not post screenshots that contain tokens, secrets, or internal endpoints.
- Do not use misleading before/after comparisons.
- Do not reuse copyrighted or unlicensed visuals.

Done checklist:
- Goal and audience defined.
- Claims verified against documentation.
- Alt text and accessibility notes written.
- A/B variants and posting plan documented.
